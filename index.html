Hello to anyone who spends time cracking or reversing obfuscated scripts. We kindly ask that you please respect our work and avoid leaking or redistributing our code. Our project has been actively maintained and updated for over 4 years, and we've built trust within the community during that time.

Our goal has always been to keep our scripts accessible and compatible with as many executors as possible. Because of this, we cannot always use the most aggressive or secure obfuscation methods — some high-security obfuscators break compatibility with low-UNC executors, which many users still rely on.

As a result, we sometimes have to use mid-level obfuscation rather than extremely heavy protection. This is purely to ensure stability and accessibility for our users. We simply ask that you respect the time and effort that has gone into this project, and avoid leaking or intentionally bypassing our protections.
